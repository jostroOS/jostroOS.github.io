Project Info

Jostro OS is a lightweight alternative to Raspberry Pi OS with multiple improvements, such as:

  • Being built off RPi OS Lite for maximum performance
  • Using IceWM for a high-performance desktop, without hogging extra system resources
  • Based on the ARM64 architecture, for future-proofing purposes and so you can get more performance out of your Pi
  • WineHQ and Box86 pre-installed so you can continue to enjoy your favourite x86 apps and games
  • Pi-Apps and PiKISS pre-installed so you can get everything started instantly without delay
  • Space Cadet Pinball pre-installed for you, retro gamers XD
